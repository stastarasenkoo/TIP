Task: Test participants were offered six tasks. For the solved the task was scored: 1/3, 2/3 or 1. (If the task is not marked as solved, then 0 points are awarded for it). All participants who passed testing were divided into four categories depending on results. The first category included participants, all solutions which were rated with a maximum score of 1. The second category included participants, all tasks of which were estimated not lower than 2/3, but it is obligatory there was at least one task, the solution of which was rated at 1. In the third category there were participants whose all tasks were rated at 2/3 points. The rest of the participants were assigned to the fourth category. Create a questionnaire for a specific test participant. The questionnaire should last name, school number, grades for solving problems. At processing the questionnaire for the participant is required to determine the amount of collected points and the category in which the participant is enrolled.
